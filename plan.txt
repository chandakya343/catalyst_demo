#### Procedure 1: Micro-Scale Educational Protocol (1 mL Toluene; ~5-10 Minutes Active Time)
This is ideal for a "dumb researcher" first run—minimal reagents, quick, low risk. From a standard organic chem lab manual; yields ~70-80% crude mixture (no isomer separation). Equipment: Basic conical vial, stir plate, separatory funnel.

**Reagents:**
- Toluene: 1.0 mL (0.86 g, 9.4 mmol)
- Conc. H₂SO₄ (98%): 1.0 mL (1.84 g, 18.4 mmol)
- Conc. HNO₃ (70%): 1.0 mL (1.12 g, 12.6 mmol)
- Diethyl ether: 8 mL (for extraction)
- 10% NaHCO₃ (aq): 10 mL (for washing)
- Anhydrous Na₂SO₄: ~1 g (drying)

**Safety Notes:** Wear gloves, goggles, lab coat. Acids are corrosive; reaction exotherms (up to 50°C if rushed). Vent separatory funnel during NaHCO₃ wash (CO₂ gas). Dispose acids as hazardous waste per local regs (e.g., CPCB guidelines).

**Steps:**
1. **Setup:** Place a 5 mL conical vial with spin vane in an ice-water bath on a stir plate. Add 1.0 mL conc. HNO₃ to the vial; stir slowly.
2. **Prepare Nitrating Mix:** Slowly add 1.0 mL conc. H₂SO₄ to the HNO₃ over 2-3 min (temp <10°C; mix turns clear/yellow).
3. **Nitration:** Add 1.0 mL toluene dropwise over 5 min with stirring (temp rises to ~20-30°C; slow if boiling starts). Remove ice bath; stir at room temp for 5 min (reaction completes; orange color).
4. **Quench/Workup:** Transfer mix to a small separatory funnel with 10 mL water. Rinse vial with 4 mL diethyl ether ×2, add to funnel.
5. **Extraction/Wash:** Shake gently, vent, separate aqueous layer (discard). Wash organic layer with 10 mL 10% NaHCO₃ (shake/vent for fizzing), then 5 mL water. Dry over Na₂SO₄ (~5 min).
6. **Isolation:** Decant ether layer to a pre-weighed vial; evaporate ether (air dry or low-heat water bath <40°C). Crude nitrotoluene: Pale yellow oil (~0.9-1.0 g). Analyze via TLC/IR if available.
7. **Troubleshooting:** Low yield? Rushed addition—redo slower. Dark color? Overheat—use more ice next time.

**Outcome:** ~0.8 g crude (ortho/para mix). For para-selectivity, chill addition to -10°C (boosts para to 45%). Scale up 10x for gram quantities.

#### Procedure 2: Bench-Scale Amateur Protocol (50 g Toluene; ~3-4 Hours Active Time)
From Sciencemadness forums (vetted by experienced hobbyists referencing industrial tweaks). Higher scale for SME validation; includes isomer separation via freezing (para crystallizes first). Yields: 60% o-nitrotoluene, 30% p-nitrotoluene (total 90% recovery).

**Reagents:**
- Toluene: 50 g (57.5 mL, 0.54 mol)
- Conc. H₂SO₄ (98%): 75 g (40.2 mL, 0.79 mol)
- Conc. HNO₃ (70%): 75 g (50.4 mL, 0.77 mol; adjust to 1.1:1 HNO₃:toluene molar ratio)
- 5% Na₂CO₃ (aq): 50 mL (washing)
- CaCl₂: ~5 g (drying)
- Petroleum ether: ~20 mL (crystal wash)

**Safety Notes:** Fume hood essential (NOx fumes carcinogenic). Exothermic—monitor temp <30°C during addition to avoid dinitro (explosive). Acids spill? Neutralize with NaHCO₃ slurry. Nitrotoluenes toxic—avoid skin contact/inhalation.

**Steps:**
1. **Setup:** In a 250 mL 2-neck RBF on magnetic stirrer/ice bath: Add 57.5 mL toluene + stir bar. Use addition funnel for acids; thermometer in side neck.
2. **Prepare Nitrating Mix:** Cool 50.4 mL HNO₃ + 40.2 mL H₂SO₄ in ice-water to <10°C (mix clears).
3. **Nitration:** Add acid mix dropwise over 30-60 min with vigorous stirring (temp <30°C; use turkey baster to refresh ice if needed). Color shifts yellow-orange.
4. **Heat/Stir:** Remove bath; let warm to 50°C (or heat gently). Hold 30-45°C for 2 hours (reflux if spattering).
5. **Quench/Separate:** Pour into sep funnel; separate top NT oil (~55 mL) from bottom acid waste (neutralize/dilute before disposal).
6. **Wash/Dry:** Wash oil with 50 mL water, 50 mL 5% Na₂CO₃ (NT sinks), 50 mL water. Dry over CaCl₂ granules (30 min), filter through cotton.
7. **Isomer Separation (Freeze Precipitation):** Chill crude (~48 g yellow oil) to -20°C (freezer or NaCl/ice bath) for 2-4 hours. Filter cold solids (p-nitrotoluene crystals) on pre-chilled Buchner; wash with cold petroleum ether. Dry at RT (mp 51°C). Filtrate = o-nitrotoluene liquid.
8. **Purify o-Isomer:** Vacuum distill filtrate (60-63°C at 1 mmHg) for pure o-nitrotoluene (bp 222°C atm).

**Yields:** o-Nitrotoluene: 30.6 g (64%); p-nitrotoluene: 12.5 g (57%, two crops). Total crude: 43 g (86%).

**Troubleshooting:** No crystals? Impure—rewash or seed with pure para. Low para? Lower temp during addition (-10°C boosts to 40%). Distillation hazard? Use short path; watch for bumping.
